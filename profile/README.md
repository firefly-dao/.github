## FireFly Protocol

Firefly Labs is a next-generation Universal Asset Interoperability Protocol designed to power seamless, secure, and intelligent cross-chain interactions in the evolving multi-chain blockchain landscape. As of late 2025, Firefly Labs delivers a comprehensive suite of solutions that bridge the gap between EVM-compatible chains, non-EVM networks, Layer-1 and Layer-2 ecosystems, enabling frictionless movement of fiat, tokens, and stablecoins across more than 70+ chains.
